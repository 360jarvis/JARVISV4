# Customer Command Center Blueprint

## Purpose

The Customer Command Center is the central intelligence hub for every customer in Jarvixx.

The goal is simple: one customer, one profile, every action connected.

## Non-Negotiable Rules

- No duplicate customer records.
- Every customer belongs to one tenant.
- Every customer action must be audit-ready.
- Customer 360 must connect bookings, quotes, payments, reviews, documents, communication, complaints, and AI insights.
- Customer data must respect role permissions.
- Customer portal visibility must be separate from internal team visibility.
